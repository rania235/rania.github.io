Habitat for Humanity of Berkeley County and Thrivent partner to help improve the lives of Hanahan family

Habitat for Humanity of Berkeley County and Thrivent have partnered together with local churches and Thrivent members in Hanahan to make a difference for the Evans' family. Habitat for Humanity of Berkeley County has been awarded $50,829 from Thrivent as part of the long-standing national partnership between Habitat and Thrivent, which helps families achieve strength, stability, and self-reliance through affordable housing. Locally, Habitat and Thrivent will partner on the construction of a home for a local family in Hanahan alongside multiple local churches in a Habitat and Thrivent Faith Builds. Over 80 of these home build projects will take place across the US. “Faith without works is dead.” -James 2:14-26. Please join us in showing our faith is working by sharing our love for our fellow man with the Thrivent Financial - Berkeley County Habitat for Humanity - Faith Build.” Adam Thayer, Thrivent Financial Professional

Habitat and Thrivent Faith Builds improve communities by helping Habitat build homes in partnership with Christian churches and families in need of decent, affordable housing in the US. Thrivent has committed to partner with Habitat, future homeowner families, Christian churches, and volunteers across the US and around the world on different housing projects. Since the national partnership’s inception in 2005 Thrivent and its members have contributed more than $252 million and more than 5.5 million volunteer hours in the U.S. and around the world to help thousands of families and individuals build strength, stability, and self-reliance through shelter. “We are truly grateful to have again been awarded the Thrivent Faith Build Grant. Thrivent Financial has not only been our largest single supporter over the last 29 years but also the largest non-government supporter of Habitat for Humanity International. Their investment in affordable housing around the world has allowed Habitat for Humanity to serve thousands of people in need. We are looking forward to the partnership with Thrivent and the faith community to serve another family in Berkeley County,” George Druyos, CEO & President of Habitat for Humanity of Berkeley County. Thrivent remains the largest non-governmental supporter of Habitat for Humanity International. For more information about the Habitat and Thrivent Partnership, visit Thrivent.com/Habitat. To learn about local volunteer opportunities, visit www.berkeleyhabitat.org/thrivent.

About Habitat for Humanity of Berkeley County HFHBC, the local affiliate of Habitat for Humanity International, is a nonprofit organization that builds strength, stability and self-reliance through affordable housing. For more information, visit www.berkeleyhabitat.org.
